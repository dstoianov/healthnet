About HealthNet

HealthNet provides a universal system for doctors, patients, nurses, and hospital administrators. Doctors can view patient medical information, create and edit prescriptions, release test results and much more. Patients can schedule meetings with their doctor, view and update their information, and view any released tests or prescriptions.

  • Mission - To provide a simple, yet powerful universal HealthCare system

HealthNet's Services

Registration

Gain access to the healthNet system in just a few clicks! In only a few minutes, your profile will be created and you will be able to add your medical information and have access to your to all of HealthNet's patient features

Appointments

See all of your appointments in one place! HealthNet's calendar view allows you to quickly see what upcoming appointments you have. Need to schedule a meeting? Our simple appointment system will have a meeting set up in no time

Prescriptions

As a Patient - Enjoy a simplified dashboard displaying all of your prescriptions - both active and expired

As a Doctor - Creating, Editing, and Viewing your prescriptions has never been easier! Your dashboard will display any prescription you have given out, with easy access to more options

Test Results

As a Patient - Easily view all released test results in a central test result dashboard

As a Doctor - Your dashboard will allow you to easily review and release test results to patients
